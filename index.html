Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025

BirdieBoard respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address (if you sign in)
  • Optional profile photos
  • Golf scores and game records
  • Device information (for security purposes via Firebase AppCheck)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To manage your user account and player profile
  • To track scores, handicaps, and tournament results
  • To protect access and detect fraud using Firebase AppCheck
  • To improve performance using anonymized analytics (Firebase Analytics)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be processed and stored by trusted third-party services, such as Firebase (by Google), used for authentication, cloud storage, and app security.

4. Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your account or data at any time by contacting us at support@birdieboard.app.
